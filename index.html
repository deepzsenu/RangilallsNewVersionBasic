Buyer's and Seller's Guidelines

To bid in RANGI LALLS Auction, you will have to be a registered buyer. For registration you have to log into RANGI LALLS Auction Website and click the button for registering. You have to fill a form and submit it. You will have to remember the user id and password which you have entered. This will be required for bidding. Your login will not be activated, and you will not be able to bid unless you pay your registration fee (Online). After the payment is made your login will be activated and you will be allowed to bid.

There is no limit to the number of auctions you can bid. So long as an auction is active, you can bid as many times as you like.

You can bid on an item by straight bidding or automatic bidding (automatic bidding is also referred to as "Auto Bid").

Enter an amount higher than the current winning bid and click the bid button. Your bid will be accepted.

Automatic bidding places bids on your behalf at pre-specified increments. Simply enter your maximum bid (the highest amount you are willing to pay), and the increment amount.

Any new bid must be greater than the current high bid. The minimum additional amount you're required to add on the previous bid is called the bid increment.

After you place a bid on the auction item page, you cannot cancel the bid. So before you bid, be very careful whether you are willing to bid or you have the sufficient fund to fulfill your bidding commitment.

The reserve price is the lowest price the seller is willing to accept for the auction item. In fact, the seller puts a reserve price for every item he posts before the starting of an auction.

During the bidding process, you will see the highest bid on the Bidding page. If your bid is the highest, the page will show you the highest bid. (This doesn't mean that you've won the auction, only that your bid is currently the highest bid. To win, the auction must close without any higher bids.)

You have to be vigilant on the auction floor. Once you find that a bid has appeared which is higher than the bid you made, you have to re-bid.

You can email the seller. In fact, it will be little required in the context of auction floated in RANGI LALLS Auction site as you have to contact RANGI LALLS with queries on any auction. RANGI LALLS acts as a facilitator of its principal (seller) for conducting its auction.

You will be informed by email when you win an auction. You will have to pay the requisite amount as material value for the bid you won to RANGI LALLS (offline). RANGI LALLS will provide you with a Delivery Order which you have to show to the seller for collecting the material.

You will be informed by email when you win an auction. You will have to pay the requisite amount as material value for the bid you won to RANGI LALLS (offline). RANGI LALLS will provide you with a Delivery Order which you have to show to the seller for collecting the material.

Payments can be made via credit card or PayPal to RANGI LALLS after winning an auction.

After paying the amount, RANGI LALLS will provide you with a Delivery Order which you have to show to the seller for collecting the material.

To register as a seller in RANGI LALLS Auction, log into RANGI LALLS Auction Website and click the button for registering as a seller. Fill in the form and submit it. You will receive a confirmation email upon successful registration.

After registering as a seller, go to the listing page and provide details of the item, including a description, starting bid, and reserve price. Submit the form to list your item for auction.

A reserve price is the minimum amount you're willing to accept for an item. Set the reserve price when listing your item to ensure it is sold for at least this amount.

You will receive an email notification once your item has been sold. The buyer will contact you for payment and delivery details.

Once your item has been sold, you will receive the buyer's contact information via email. Use this information to arrange payment and delivery details with the buyer.

You will receive payment from the buyer directly once the auction has ended and the buyer has been notified. Ensure all payment details are clear before shipping the item.

Arrange shipping with the buyer once payment has been confirmed. Use a reliable shipping service and provide the buyer with tracking information to ensure a smooth transaction.

If the buyer doesn't pay within the agreed timeframe, contact RANGI LALLS support for assistance. We will help mediate the situation and ensure a fair resolution.

If your item doesn't sell, you can relist it by going to your seller's dashboard and selecting the relist option. Update any details as necessary and submit the form to relist your item.
